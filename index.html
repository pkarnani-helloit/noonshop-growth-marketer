Noon Shop  ·  Role Profile

Growth Marketer Position

A clear picture of the role, the mindset, and the responsibilities that turn website traffic into revenue, and how to tell the right candidate from the wrong one.

1

The Purpose of This Role

Why the position exists

Drive qualified traffic to NoonShop.com and convert more of those visitors into customers.

This is a growth role focused on customer acquisition, conversion optimization, and marketing performance. It is not a content-creation or social-media-management role. The person continually answers two questions:

Question OneHow do we get more qualified visitors to the website?
Question TwoHow do we convert more of those visitors into customers?

Success is measured by: increased traffic · higher conversion rates · lower acquisition cost · improved retention · growth in revenue.

2

The Type of Marketer We Want

How they think about the business

A practical, results-oriented Internet / Growth Marketer who understands how ecommerce businesses grow online and can connect every piece into one cohesive strategy. They think in terms of:

Traffic Funnels Conversions Customer Acquisition Cost Return on Ad Spend Lifetime Value Retention Attribution Automation Data

In one line

Part strategist, part analyst, part technologist, part marketer.

They don't just run campaigns; they build systems that continuously improve results.
3

What This Person Actually Does

Seven core areas of responsibility
📈
Acquisition

Traffic Generation

  • Paid Search: Google Ads, Shopping, Performance Max
  • Paid Social: Meta, TikTok, Pinterest, YouTube
  • Audience targeting, retargeting & lookalikes
  • Bid management, budget allocation, ad testing
  • Understands CTR, CPC, ROAS, CAC
🔍
Organic

SEO & Organic Growth

  • Keyword research & search visibility
  • Product & category page optimization
  • Technical SEO recommendations
  • Content strategy that drives search traffic
  • Goal: steadily improve visibility, not become a specialist
🎯
Conversion

Conversion Rate Optimization

  • Studies where visitors enter, exit & abandon
  • Tackles cart & checkout abandonment
  • Heat mapping & A/B testing
  • Tools: GA4, Microsoft Clarity, Hotjar, Lucky Orange
  • Answers why aren't visitors buying?
🔁
Recovery

Retargeting & Customer Recapture

  • Cart & browse abandonment campaigns
  • Product-view & dynamic retargeting ads
  • Abandoned-cart & win-back email sequences
  • Facebook & Google display retargeting
  • Often the fastest way to grow revenue without more traffic
✉️
Retention

Email, CRM & Automation

  • Customer segmentation & lifecycle marketing
  • Automated flows & trigger-based campaigns
  • Re-engagement & repeat-purchase campaigns
  • Platforms: Klaviyo, Mailchimp, HubSpot, Shopify Flow, Zapier
  • Maximizes value of existing customers
🛠️
Funnels

Funnel Development

  • Lead magnets: buying guides, checklists, email offers
  • Nurture funnels: welcome series, trust building
  • Customer funnels: upsell, cross-sell, loyalty
  • Understands visitors rarely buy on first visit
📊
Measurement

Data, Analytics & Technical Fluency

  • Traffic: sessions, users, source/medium, organic vs. paid
  • Engagement: bounce rate, time on site, pages/session
  • Conversion: conversion rate, cart & checkout abandonment
  • Advertising: CTR, CPC, CPA, ROAS, CAC
  • Customer: repeat rate, lifetime value, retention
  • Technical: GA4, Tag Manager, pixels, event tracking, UTMs, Shopify

Makes decisions based on data, not opinions, and works effectively with developers without being dependent on them. (Not a developer or designer.)

The Simplest Definition

We are not looking for a social media manager, content creator, graphic designer, or marketing coordinator.

We want a growth-focused Internet Marketer who can attract visitors, understand their behavior, optimize their journey, recover lost opportunities, leverage technology and automation, and systematically turn traffic into revenue.
